About

My name is Ouirich and I am an artist based in Vancouver, BC. My discipline of choice is lighting.

My work as a digital artist includes visual effects credits in feature films, animated tv shows and commercials.

Currently working at Wildbrain as lighting lead on Sonic Prime. Some studios I worked with:

  • Sony Picture Imageworks (Vancouver)
  • Animal Logic (Sydney)
  • Cinesite (London)
  • Dneg (London)
  • Mac Guff (Paris)

I am currently shooting with a Fujifilm XT-3. Equipped with the lenses Fujinon XF35mm F2, XF18-55mm F28-4 and Samyang 12mm F2, I mostly shot landscape and street photography. I have been dabbling in portrait recently and plan to keep working on it.
